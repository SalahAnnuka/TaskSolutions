[Difference between <article> and <p>]

<article>:
    - has a complete meaning even when removed from context of the original page.
    - usualy used for large text (can also contain multiple paragraphs).
    - used as main text in news articles and blogs.
    - semantic block for defining its content as topic or article

<p>:
    - its content can be dependant on the context of the page
    - used for simple paragraphs of text
    - is the general tag for text
    - does not have a specific semantic meaning

[Difference between <section> and <div>]

<section>:
    - has a semantic meaning
    - used to organise elements that are related to each other

<div>:
    - is a regular tag for many purposes
